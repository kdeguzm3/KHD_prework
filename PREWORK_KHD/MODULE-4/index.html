Against The Current

Against The Current is an American Rock Band originating in New York. Formed in 2011, the band quickly gained a sizable online following after posting covers of popular songs on Youtube.

Members
Vocalist Chrizzy Costanza
Guitarist Dan Gow
Drummer Will Ferri
